Welcome

Heyhoe Garden Design has been creating gardens in Rockland and Westchester Counties in New York and Bergen County in New Jersey for 25 years.

We offer a free consultation. We work closely with clients to ensure their wishes and aesthetic concerns are realized. Our best projects are collaborative. We build gardens of all sizes and for all purposes.

We design, install and maintain Perennial Borders, Foundation Plantings, Screening, Arbors, Decks, Fencing, Rustic patios, Walkways and Walls.
